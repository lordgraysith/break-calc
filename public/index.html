Reset your hours

Effective July 1st 2013- A driver may restart a 7/8 consecutive day period after taking 34 or more consecutive hours off duty. Must include two periods from 1 a.m. to 5 a.m. home terminal time, and may only be used once per week, or 168 hours, measured from the beginning of the previous restart.

Source - https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/rules-regulations/topics/hos/index.htm
